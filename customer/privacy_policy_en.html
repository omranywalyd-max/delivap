Privacy Policy

Deliv App | Last updated: July 7, 2026

Introduction

At Deliv, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Deliv app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email, phone number, gender
  • Location: Your precise location for delivery addresses and nearby stores
  • Saved Locations: Addresses you save (home, work, etc.)
  • Profile Photo: Photo you upload or from Google account
  • Order Data: Products, prices, delivery addresses, notes
  • Comments: Public comments about drivers (shown with your name and photo)
  • Device Token (FCM): For push notifications

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide delivery, transport, and other services
  • To show stores and products relevant to your location
  • To connect your orders with suitable drivers
  • To send notifications about your order status
  • To improve our services and user experience

Data Sharing with Drivers

When you place an order, the assigned driver can see:

  • Your first name
  • Your phone number (for delivery coordination)
  • Your delivery location

You can hide your phone number from the privacy settings in the app.

Public Comments

Comments you post about drivers are public and visible to other users with your name and photo. You can delete your comments or report inappropriate ones.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

  • Access and update your data anytime from the profile page
  • Request deletion of your account and data (available in account settings)
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Report inappropriate content

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us through the app's help section or via email available in the app.
